About The Cloud Restaurant

From humble beginnings...

Look up into the sky. What do you see? That's right. Clouds. The idea behind our restaurant was hatched on the craziest cloud day of all time. I mean the sky was literally just going ham, and the clouds were a sight to behold.

On that fateful day, I took a sizeable bite out of my burger and looked up at the clouds. A friend of mine (the now co-owner of The Cloud Restaurant) took a bite out of his burrito at the same time and said, "Wow. It feels like we're on those clouds. How cool would that be?"

It was then that we decided to open up our own restaurant that you are now enjoying. Bon Apetit!
