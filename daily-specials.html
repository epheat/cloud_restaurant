Authentic Gyros

Our Gyros are so delicious, your tastebuds will explode on contact. Imagine the best home cooking you've ever had, and then multiply that taste by 100,000. Only at that point can you even begin to understand how savory and absolutely mouthwatering our Gyros are.

These Gyros are made from fresh lamb, hand cut iceberg lettuce, ripe grown tomatoes, our signature Greek sauce, and soft pita bread. You will want to lick your plate clean just to pick up any moresel that may have been left behind while you chowed down on your Gyro. We guarantee that you'll be on cloud 9 while you eat!
